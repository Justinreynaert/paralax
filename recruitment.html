So what's Essence?

Essence was formed during Highmaul and we quickly established ourselves as one of the top 2 days a week guilds. We are a group of skilled players that clear mythic content in an efficient manner with a positive and respectful atmosphere. We play smarter, not harder, by quickly identifying problems and iterating a raid strategy.

We have a small but active roster, so if we recruit you then it will always be for a spot in progression. Our members take an active part in making the most of our short 6 hour raiding week by assuming responsibility in raids, contributing improvements to raid strategies, and always looking to improve themselves.

Cool story, what do you expect of me?

  • Independent: you take intiative by making cetain calls or coming up with creative refinements to raid strategy
  • Self-driven: you strive to improve yourself without needing external criticism.
  • Non-elitist: we expect you to maintain a mature and respectful attitude at all times, and vice versa from us.
  • Team player: you understand that the guild comes first.
  • Skilled: you are able to play your class and handle raid mechanics, backed up by logs.
  • Prepared: you study each encounter and contribute to/read the forum discussions beforehand.
  • Active: we have a small roster, near 100% attendance is required.
  • Reliable: you have a stable internet connection and computer
